Proud to put you,
the student first.

At the University of Bolton, we pride ourselves on our student-focused approach to learning, supporting
your career ambitions, and, quite simply, ensuring you enjoy your time at University; through quality teaching, passionate lecturers, supportive environments, great facilities, and on graduation, helping you find a job.

Why Diagnostic, Intervention and Support System?

Have you decided to pursue the career of your dream but you have no required qualifications? Once you have made a decision to join the university, it can be
daunting to find out where to start. We are here to help!

Diagnostic, Intervention and Support System helps students from various backgrounds
to acquire necessary skills to fulfill their academic potential. DISS analyses your skills and knowledge, making it easy to recommend supplementary courses that will be the best fit for you. What are you waiting for? Join today!
